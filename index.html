Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about E-DERA

What data format does E-DERA accept?

E-DERA accepts CSV files with transaction data including date, description, and amount columns. We support both sales and expenses data. The system automatically cleans and normalizes your data during upload.

How accurate are the forecasts?

Our ensemble of 9 ML models achieves 94% forecast accuracy on average. Accuracy improves with more historical data (minimum 3 months recommended). The system provides confidence intervals for each prediction.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally on your machine. We never upload your CSV files to external servers, and session data is cleared when you close your browser. Our code is open-source for full transparency.

What's the difference between Standard and Advanced Analysis?

Standard Analysis uses traditional financial ratios and basic forecasting. Advanced Analysis activates all 9 deep learning models including DA-GRU, TFT, N-BEATS, DeepAR, Deep Autoencoder, Isolation Forest, GAT, TabNet, and our Ensemble Meta-Model for comprehensive risk scoring.

Can I integrate E-DERA with my accounting software?

Currently, E-DERA accepts CSV exports from any accounting system (QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, etc.). API integration for automated data sync is on our roadmap for Q2 2025.

Is this really free for educational use?

Yes! E-DERA is a student research project and completely free for educational purposes. We built this to demonstrate advanced ML techniques for financial risk assessment. For production use by businesses, please contact us for licensing.
